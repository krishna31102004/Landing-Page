Intro

Hello, I'm Krishna Balaji. Originally from Mumbai, India, I was born on Halloween in 2004. My educational journey began at JBCN International School, where I completed my studies from 1st through 12th grade. I’m now a junior at Arizona State University, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Business.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I’ve had the honor of delivering a TED Talk, which you can watch here. I also took part in a fundraising initiative as part of the IAYP program in 2020, successfully raising INR 20,464 to support our cause.

Projects

I've worked on a variety of projects that span across technology and business. Here are some highlights:

  • Pantry Tracker - Developed a smart inventory management system using Next.js, Material UI, and Firebase. This project, deployed on Vercel with CI/CD pipelines, integrates AI features using OpenAI and leverages Google Cloud Platform (GCP) for robust backend services, enhancing inventory tracking and user experience.
  • Disease Prediction System - Created a machine learning model using Python to predict diseases based on patient data. The system utilizes algorithms such as logistic regression, decision trees, and random forests, achieving high accuracy in disease predictions.
  • Jirani Digital Library Software - Led the development of an SQL-backed online database with an intuitive front-end interface. This platform serves educational resources to underprivileged communities in Kenya. The project was pitched in a competition and secured over $3,000 in funding.

For more insights into these projects and their technical details, feel free to explore my blog.
